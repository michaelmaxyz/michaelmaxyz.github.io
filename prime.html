Prime Number Visualizations

I made a bunch of visualizations for Prime Numbers. There is a integer counter that constantly goes up by 1, and when the counter is prime, the current pixel iterates through the RGB wheel by 1 and changes direction by 135 degrees. (By default). Creates some very interesting/unpredictable behaviors. Is this useful at all? Probably not, but it created some weird spirals!
